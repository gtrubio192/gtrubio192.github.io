About Gabe

Gabe is a front-end developer and designer hailing from Austin, Texas - yes, he is indeed like the last of the Mohicans, or a fabled unicorn. He thrives on creating interactive and intuitive digital experiences. As he sharpens his skills in coding and design, the results are an ever growing showcase of how far he has come in developing fullstack Javascript web applications.

Some of the coding assets in his toolbelt inlcude:

  • Javascript (Angular, React, Backbone, Ember)
  • HTML5
  • CSS3 (Twitter Bootstrap, SASS, Bourbon.io)
  • Mobile and Responsive Design

His passion and talent are seen in the work he has done for clients including and not limited to FWD.us, Texas Department of Transportation, and Total Roofing. Through these experiences and with the helpful talent at The Iron Yard, Gabe has grown into a versatile developer and designer.
