Our Products

We offer the highest grade cannabis concentrates

Cannabis Shatter

Shatter, with its flawless amber glass transparency, has a reputation for being the purest and cleanest type of extract.

Cannabis Wax

Cannabis wax refers to the softer, opaque oils that have lost their transparency after extraction. Unlike those of transparent oils, the molecules of cannabis wax crystallize as a result of agitation.

Live Resin

Live resin uses fresh, frozen flowers that are taken from the grow before they have had the chance to cure and dry. It is very similar to shatter, both of which are a butane hash-oil (BHO)
